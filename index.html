ABOUT MYST

Welcome to the electrifying world of Myst, an alternative rock band led by a forceful lineup of talented women. With our debut album, Without, we're ready to set the music scene ablaze, fusing captivating melodies, poetic lyrics, and raw energy into an unforgettable sonic experience. Rooted in the rich legacy of alternative rock, our music embodies a fearless spirit, challenging conventions and embracing individuality. Through our songs, we explore themes of loss, resilience, and the power of self-discovery.

As a woman-led band, we are here to make a statement. We stand as a symbol of empowerment, showing that passion, talent, and determination know no boundaries. Our music is a call to all who dare to dream, encouraging them to find their own voice and break free from the constraints society imposes.

Welcome to the revolution. Together, let's rise and rock the world!
